Hazirlanma tarihi: 5 Mart 2026

Bu dokuman Google Play ve App Store icin kopyala-yapistir metadata, screenshot metinleri ve 30 gunluk ASO aksiyon plani icerir.

## 1) Resmi Limitler (Kaynakli)

Google Play (Store listing)
- App name: en fazla 30 karakter.
- Short description: en fazla 80 karakter.
- Full description: en fazla 4000 karakter.
Kaynak: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/13393723

Google Play (Graphic assets)
- Telefon screenshot: en az 2, en fazla 8.
- Cozunurluk: 16:9 veya 9:16 oran, min 320 px, max 3840 px.
- Icon: 512x512 PNG.
- Feature graphic: 1024x500 PNG/JPG.
Kaynak: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/1078870

App Store Connect (Metadata)
- Name: max 30 karakter.
- Subtitle: max 30 karakter.
- Promotional text: max 170 karakter.
- Keywords: max 100 byte.
- Description: max 4000 karakter.
Kaynak: https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/reference/required-localizable-and-editable-properties/

App Store Connect (Screenshots)
- iPhone screenshot setinde 6.5" veya 6.9" boyutundan biri zorunlu.
Kaynak: https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/reference/app-platform-and-device-compatibility/
